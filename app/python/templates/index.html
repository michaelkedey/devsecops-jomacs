DevOps and DevSecOps – Final Projects

Note:

  1. The purpose of this assessment is to evaluate your ability to integrate security practices into the software development and operations lifecycle, ensure adherence to SOC 2 compliance standards, and effectively apply DevSecOps principles to deliver secure and compliant solutions.
  2. You will demonstrate your proficiency in designing and implementing security-focused workflows, automating compliance checks, and documenting your processes to meet industry-standard security and compliance requirements.
  3. Please complete all the five outlined tasks to demonstrate your DevOps, DevSecOps and SOC 2–aligned approaches. Focus on setting up, testing, and documenting your solution so that the key security and compliance aspects are clear (e.g., relevant artifacts, logs, or policy checks). You can also focus on doing this in groups.
  4. There are tons of projects in GitHub or the open internet that you can use as your source code for these projects.
  5. Deploy single containers into an ECS Fargate cluster, multiple containers into EKS and non-containerized applications into an EC2 instance or elastic beanstalk or lambda functions.
  6. You can find detailed instructions on how to submit your completed work near the end of this document.

Projects:

  • Secure CI/CD Pipeline with Automated Scanning
  • Local Infrastructure-as-Code with Policy Enforcement
  • Container Scanning & Registry Compliance
  • Centralized Logging & Security Monitoring
  • Lightweight Incident Response “Runbook” + Security Dashboard
